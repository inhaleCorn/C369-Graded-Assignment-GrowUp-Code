Regulatory Sandbox

The Regulatory Sandbox provides a safe environment for financial institutions to experiment with innovative financial products or services without immediately meeting all regulatory requirements. This helps promote innovation while ensuring consumer protection.

As an investor, it's important to understand that the sandbox helps bring new financial products to market safely. Growup adheres to these regulations by ensuring that any new products we introduce go through rigorous testing and comply with all necessary guidelines.

Anti-Money Laundering

Anti-money laundering (AML) regulations are critical for preventing financial crimes. These regulations require financial institutions to implement measures to detect and report suspicious activities that may involve money laundering or terrorism financing.

As an investor, it's important to know that AML regulations help protect the financial system from abuse. Growup follows strict AML guidelines to ensure the integrity of our services and safeguard against financial crimes.
