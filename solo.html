Investing Solo

Choose order type:

Market order

A market order is a buy or sell order to be executed immediately at current market prices.

Limit order

A limit order is a type of order to purchase or sell a security at a specified price or better.

GTC order

A GTC (Good 'Til Canceled) order remains active until you decide to cancel it.

Stop loss order

A stop-loss order is designed to limit an investor's loss on a security position.
